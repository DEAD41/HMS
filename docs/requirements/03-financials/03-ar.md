# Accounts Receivable / Patient & Insurance

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Module | FIN |
| Sub-module | AR |
| Status | Draft — pending verification |
| Source | §5.1 AR; §5.2 A order-to-cash |

## 1. Scope
Track patient and insurer receivables from HMS billing, receipts, denials, and aging.

## 2. Exclusions
External collection agency system.

## 3. Actors and Permissions
- AR Officer
- Cashier (HMS payments)
- Finance Controller

## 4. Functional Requirements
| ID | Requirement |
|---|---|
| FIN-AR-FR-001 | Create AR open items from finalized invoices/claims. |
| FIN-AR-FR-002 | Apply receipts and allocations. |
| FIN-AR-FR-003 | Track insurance claim settlements/partials/denials. |
| FIN-AR-FR-004 | Provide aging analysis for patients and insurers. |
| FIN-AR-FR-005 | Support write-offs under approval. |

## 5. Workflow and State Transitions
Receivable: `Open -> PartiallySettled -> Settled | WrittenOff`.
ClaimAR follows HMS claim statuses with financial settlement states.

## 6. Data / Entities and Validation
- `Receivable`
- `ReceiptApplication`
- `Denial`
- `WriteOff`

## 7. Business Rules
| ID | Rule |
|---|---|
| FIN-AR-BR-001 | Receipts cannot over-apply without credit balance rules. |
| FIN-AR-BR-002 | Write-off above threshold needs approval. |

## 8. Approvals
Write-offs and courtesy discounts via FND approvals.

## 9. APIs and Module Ownership
**Owner:** FIN

### APIs
- `POST /api/fin/ar/open-items/sync`
- `POST /api/fin/ar/receipts/apply`
- `POST /api/fin/ar/write-offs`

### Events Published
- `ReceivableSettled`
- `ArWriteOffPosted`

### Events Consumed
- `InvoiceFinalized`
- `ClaimSubmitted`
- `PaymentReceived`
- `DischargeCompleted`

## 10. Notifications
- Denial alerts
- High aging alerts

## 11. Reports
- AR aging
- Collection efficiency
- Denial reasons

## 12. Audit, Retention, and Privacy
Patient financial PHI/PII minimized; access restricted; retention statutory.

## 13. Failure, Idempotency, and Concurrency
- Application idempotent by receiptId+line
- Concurrency on open item balance

## 14. Non-Functional Requirements
- Aging query P95 < 2s for facility month

## 15. Dependencies
- HMS Billing/Claims
- FIN GL/Cash

## 16. Acceptance Criteria
| ID | Criterion |
|---|---|
| FIN-AR-AC-001 | Finalized invoice creates AR open item. |
| FIN-AR-AC-002 | Aging report shows unpaid insurer balances. |

## 17. Open Assumptions
- Customer master for insurers may overlap payer records from HMS.
